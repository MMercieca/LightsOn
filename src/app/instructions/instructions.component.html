This is a web implementation of the Lights Out game. All the boards are winnable, I promise.

Each button, or square, has two states: on (yellow), and off (blue). The goal is to turn all of the buttons on. Pressing a button will cause it to toggle from on to off or off to on. It will also toggle the square above, below, to the left, and to right.

To make sure that each game can be won, the app will start with a completed board. It will then randomly toggle buttons before presenting it to the user. Since this started with a completed board, the resulting board can be won by at least one path--- that it, doing what the app did in reverse.

Easy, medium, and hard games vary in board size and the number of randomizing moves.

Level Board Size Number of Moves to Randomize
Easy 5 x 5 5
Medium 10 x 10 5
Hard 10 x 10 7
